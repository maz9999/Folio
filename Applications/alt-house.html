Based in Melbourne, Australia, Maria is a mixed-media designer whose practice involves lots of play and banana bread. She enjoys challenging traditional conventions and talking in the third person.

Currently: Designer at Raft
Previous: Web development course at Academy Xi, Designer at Today, Internship at Ignite Online

FYA Magazine

FYA is a monthly Paris-based magazine that celebrates the upcoming generation of creatives. Shining a light on those who break stereotypes as well as exploring the hidden gems in cinema and music. The bold typography references the audacious characters of the artists interviewed.

Year: 2021
Creative direction in collabration with: Sofya

Sakura

Guided by the principle of Wabi Sabi, Sakura is one of the newest sushi train restaurants to grace the re-developments of the Wesley Place building in the Melbourne CBD. Screen-printed flower imagery draws upon traditional Japanese woodblock printing, ukiyo-e.

Studio: Raft
Year: 2021
