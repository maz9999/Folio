Based in Melbourne, Australia, Maria is a mixed-media designer whose practice involves lots of play and banana bread. She enjoys challenging traditional conventions and talking in the third person.

Currently: Upskilling in a Junior Web development course with Academy Xi

Are.na

Across the seas

Across the seas explores the intersection between personal and national migrant identities. How are migrant’s shaped by the countries that they move to and are their identities accurately reflected in the nation’s narrative?

Typeface: Authentic sans, Times New Roman, Maison Neue
Year: 2020

Instagram Market

Instagram Market is a sustainable marketplace aimed at targeting the youth. The campaign leverages Instagram’s existing brand to achieve a vibrant and contemporary aesthetic, placing heightened value on our relationship to the product rather than its materialistic attributes.

Typeface: Neue Haas Grotesque, Cardinal Fruit
Year: 2020
In collobration with: Alex Darbyshire

Transcendence

Transcendence is a project driven by the desire to shift perceptions of what traditional art or design can be. To turn the undesirable desirable through an immersive Augmented reality installation, exhibiting 8 digital sculptures at the Queen Victoria Gardens.

Typeface: Voyage
Year: 2020

Savers

A re-brand of the opportunity shop Savers which aims to shift attitudes and negative connotations towards the nature of shopping second-hand by incorporating a high fashion into slow fashion outlook.

Typeface: Editorial New, Labil Grotesque
Year: 2019

Emma

Inspired by the warmth and radiance of a sunflower, Emma James is a boutique jewellery label that carves new meaning to luxury within hand-made, sustainable and unique creations.

Year: 2020

FYA Magazine

FYA is a monthly Paris-based magazine that celebrates the upcoming generation of creatives. Shining a light on those who break stereotypes as well as exploring the hidden gems in cinema and music. The bold typography references the audacious nature of these risk-taking youths.

Typeface: PVC Menu, Neue Montreal
Year: 2021
In collobration with: Sofya
